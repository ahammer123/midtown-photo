Edit text the easy way

  1. Load. If you opened this from your site (…/admin.html), your list loads automatically. Otherwise click Open file… and choose data.json from the equipment folder.
  2. Edit. Change the page header (title, notice, captions), then sections, subsections, and each row (qty, item, price).
  3. Save. Click Save changes — your browser downloads data.json. Replace the old file next to index.html (drag into the folder on your Mac or NAS), then refresh the list page.
